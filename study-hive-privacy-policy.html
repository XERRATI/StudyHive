Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 23, 2026

The short version

Study Hive doesn't have accounts, doesn't have a server database, and doesn't run analytics or ad trackers. Everything you enter — your countdown, tasks, flashcards, habits, streaks, and settings — is saved directly in your own browser and never sent to us. The only thing that ever reaches us is what you choose to type into the feedback form.

What we store

Study Hive runs entirely as a single page in your browser. Your data (goal name and date, focus sessions, flashcard decks, to‑dos, habits, streaks, garden, and preferences like sleep mode) is saved using your browser's local storage on your device only. We never receive, see, or store this information ourselves, and it never leaves your device. Clearing your browser's site data, using a different browser, or switching devices will reset it, since nothing is backed up on our end.

The feedback form

If you submit the "Talk to the Hive" feedback form, we receive only what you type and, if you choose to provide it, your email address so we can reply. Depending on how the site is configured, this is delivered either through your own email app (a normal email you send us) or through a small form service. We use this solely to read and respond to feedback, and to improve Study Hive. We don't add you to a mailing list or share this with third parties.

Fonts

Study Hive loads its typefaces (Fredoka and Baloo 2) from Google Fonts. Loading a font makes a request to Google's servers, which may log standard technical information like your IP address, subject to Google's own privacy policy. We don't control or receive this data ourselves.

Cookies & tracking

Study Hive does not set its own cookies, does not use analytics, and does not use advertising or third‑party trackers.

Children's privacy

Study Hive is meant to be usable by students of any age without creating an account or providing personal information. We don't knowingly collect personal information from anyone. If you're a parent or guardian and have a concern, please reach out through the feedback form.

Changes to this policy

If this policy changes, we'll update the date above. Continuing to use Study Hive after a change means you're okay with the updated version.

Contact

Questions about this policy? Use the "Talk to the Hive" feedback form on the landing page — it comes straight to us.
