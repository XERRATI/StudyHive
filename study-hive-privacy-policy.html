🐝 Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 22, 2026
Plain-language summary: Study Hive runs entirely in your browser. Your countdown, tasks, flashcards, habits, streaks, and other app data are stored locally on your own device — they are not sent to us. The only thing we ever receive is what you choose to type into the Feedback form.

1. What this policy covers

This policy describes how Study Hive ("the app," "we," "us") handles information for anyone who uses the app or the website it's hosted on.

2. Information stored on your device

Study Hive uses your browser's local storage to save things like your goal countdown, focus session history, to-do list, flashcards, habits, streaks, water tracker, and similar app data. This information stays on your device and in your browser — we do not have a server that receives or stores it, and we cannot see it.

Clearing your browser data, using a different browser, or using a different device will reset this information, since nothing is synced anywhere else at this time.

3. Information you choose to send us

If you use the Feedback tool, we receive whatever you type into the message box, plus your email address only if you choose to provide one (it's optional). We use this solely to read your feedback and, if you left an email, to reply to you. We don't use it for marketing and we don't sell it.

4. Cookies and tracking

Study Hive does not use advertising cookies or third-party trackers. If we add basic, privacy-respecting analytics in the future (for example, to see which features are used), we'll update this policy first.

5. Children's privacy

Study Hive is designed to be useful for students, including those under 18. We don't knowingly collect personal information from children beyond what's described above (locally-stored app data, and optional feedback you choose to submit). If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child has submitted personal information to us through the Feedback form, contact us using that same form and we'll delete it.

6. Third-party services

The Feedback form may be processed through a third-party form-handling service so we can receive messages by email. That provider's own privacy policy governs how they handle the transmission of that message.

7. Your choices

  • You can clear all locally-stored app data at any time by clearing your browser's site data for this page.
  • You can use the app without ever submitting feedback — nothing is sent to us unless you choose to use that form.

8. Changes to this policy

If Study Hive adds accounts, cloud sync, or other features that change what data we handle, we'll update this page and the "last updated" date above.

9. Contact

Questions about this policy? Use the Feedback tool in the app, or in the site footer, and mention it's about privacy.
